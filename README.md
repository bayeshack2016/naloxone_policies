# Naloxone Regulations Effects

Measuring causal effect of naloxone regulation policies on drug abuse deaths

## Prompt

The abuse and addiction to opioids such as heroin, morphine, and prescription pain relievers is one of the most serious health problems in the US today. An estimated 2.1 million people in the US suffer from substance use disorders related to prescription opioid pain relievers in 2012 and an estimated 467,000 addicted to heroin.
